BuenaVista Family Adventure

About

If you're looking for a day packed with excitement, nature, and a bit of relaxation, the Buenavista Family Adventure is the perfect escape. We've done this tour ourselves, and let me tell you—it's a full day of fun for everyone! You’ll start with a walk across hanging bridges that weave through the jungle canopy, giving you a bird’s-eye view of the forest. The bridges offer a peaceful way to take in the lush greenery, and if you're lucky, you might spot some wildlife along the way!

Then, it’s time to amp things up with the zip-lining adventure—10 cables that zip you through the treetops! There’s nothing quite like the rush of flying over the forest, with the world below you and the wind in your face.

Next, prepare for a wild ride down a 400-yard jungle waterslide. It’s fast, exhilarating, and a great way to cool off after the morning's adventures.

After all that excitement, you'll be more than ready for the deluxe lunch buffet. We’ve tried it, and there’s something for everyone—lots of variety and super tasty!

For the afternoon, you have two options: take a leisurely horseback ride through the countryside or opt for a hike to a waterfall if you’re more in the mood for a nature walk. Both options offer a serene break from the morning's thrills.

And to top it all off, you’ll wrap up the day with some well-deserved relaxation at the volcanic sauna, mud bath, and natural hot springs. It’s the perfect way to unwind, soothe any sore muscles, and let the day’s excitement settle in.

This adventure has a little bit of everything—adrenaline, nature, great food, and relaxation. It’s a day that truly offers something for everyone in the family.

Frequently asked questions

What should I wear?

Comfort is key! Wear light, comfortable clothes that you can move around in, and sturdy shoes for walking and hiking. You’ll also want to pack a swimsuit and flip-flops for the hot springs and waterslide.

How long does the tour last?

t’s a full-day adventure! Including the 2-hour drive each way, you’ll want to set aside most of the day for this tour. Trust us, the day flies by with all the activities packed in.

Do I need to bring food or drinks?

No need! The tour includes a delicious lunch buffet with lots of options to choose from. Just bring a water bottle to stay hydrated throughout the day.

Is this tour suitable for kids?

Absolutely! This is a great family adventure, with activities for all ages. The guides are super helpful and make sure everyone feels safe and comfortable.

What’s the deal with the horseback riding option?

The horseback riding is a fun, easy-going ride through the countryside. It lasts about 40 minutes and is a great way to enjoy the natural beauty of the area. If horseback riding isn’t your thing, you can opt for a hike to a nearby waterfall instead.

How do I prepare for the zip-lining?

The guides will give you all the instructions and gear you need. You don’t need any prior experience—just a sense of adventure! And if you’re feeling a bit nervous, don’t worry, the guides will make sure you’re safe and comfortable the whole time.

What about the volcanic sauna and mud bath?

After all that activity, you’ll get to relax in a volcanic sauna, try out a mud bath, and soak in the natural hot springs. It’s an amazing way to unwind and let your body relax after a full day of adventure.

Is the tour private?

Yes! This is a private tour, so you’ll be enjoying the experience with just your group and the guide, which makes it even more special.

What if it rains?

Costa Rica’s weather can be unpredictable, but a little rain won’t stop the fun. Most activities continue rain or shine, but if the weather becomes unsafe, the tour will be adjusted accordingly. You’ll still have a fantastic day!

What is the cancellation policy?

Since we’re not the tour operators, it’s best to check directly with the company for any cancellation policies. We’ll connect you with them, and they’ll take care of any changes or refunds.
